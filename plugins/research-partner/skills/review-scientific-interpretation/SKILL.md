---
name: review-scientific-interpretation
description: Use when claims, interpretation, bias, or generalizability must be reviewed against the actual cohort, design, and measurements.
---

Use this skill for the `scientific-reviewer` lane.

Required output contract:
- `Scope`
- `Artifact map / evidence reviewed`
- `Findings`
- `Direct evidence vs inference`
- `Required tests / checks`
- `Recommended actions`

Always classify the main claims as:
- descriptive
- associational
- causal
- predictive/prognostic

Assess whether interpretation is overstated because of selection, ascertainment, measurement error, or narrow cohort structure.
